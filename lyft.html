Lyft demo

2019 • UI and motion • California

Team

  • Oscar DeLeon, Designer
  • Eben Maglic, Developer

Showcasing color and motion


We built a lyft demo leveraging their bright colors and marketing campaign

Goal

Build a fun and magnetic mobile ad to drive new consumer traffic.

Challenges

• Campaign was promotional so it had to be enticing
• Had to be a vertical ad

Approach

Luckily for me, Lyft already has a recognizable and bold color scheme (it doesn’t get any more noticeable than hot pink) which did half the work for me. From there, I knew I had to focus on making it alluring, not just with the Lyft credit promotion, but I needed to make sure they were tapping and signing up. I ended up creating a simple, clean design which intuitively directed new users to tap for credit and then immediately sign up to obtain the code. I grabbed attention and kept it by using lively motion design and micro-interactions.
