Yahoo Finance App

2017 • App campaign • California

Team

  • Oscar DeLeon, Designer
  • Danny Spyra, Sr. Product manager
  • Cary Codi, Producer

Showing young people finance can be fun


Led design on all ads and emails for Yahoo’s Finance app campaign.

Goals

To move away from the current demographic and make it fun, colorful, and appealing for a younger generation.

Challenge

Conflicting messages from two sides of the stakeholders: One side wanted to overhaul and target young people, and the other thought that the current older demographic and subsequent design were fine

Aproach

Yahoo Creative Studio had some great ideas coming down the pipeline, and had actually just finished a fantastic new campaign featuring Warren Buffett. Even though I loved the Buffett campaign, I felt it was a little tame compared to where I knew we could take ours. I took inspiration from the shapes and colors, and then translated that into the ads for Yahoo Finance. If we were going to attract a new generation of users, we needed to take a risk.
