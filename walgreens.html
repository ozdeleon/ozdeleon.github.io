Walgreens

2019 • Awareness campaign • California

Team

  • Oscar DeLeon, Designer
  • Melissa Merril, Producer

Freedom to create awareness


With Walgreens being such a large company, I was excited to have been given the opportunity to completely create an ad for their Medicare campaign, all the way from concept to finished product.

Goal

The main goal was to build an informative and fun ad that felt cohesive with other Walgreens campaigns, all while keeping the ad distinct.

Challenges

I had a few obstacles to contend with right out of the gate:

• No definitive direction (which I prefer, if I’m being honest! 😀)

• Wanted the ad to have an emphasis on turning 65, but without a birthday theme as they already had one running at the time

• No assets

Aproach

Since this was a “from the ground up” type of project, I proposed a few ideas and created storyboards right away.

Storyboard

I knew I wanted it to be clear, concise, and have any easy flow to it since I also had to make sure the enrollment plan information was crystal clear. Out of my ideas, a simple concept was chosen where we see a balloon with a “65” on the front, break away from the pack, and float upwards past crucial reminders of when and how to apply for Medicare Part D.

Walgreens loved it, and once I was given the green light, I devised, designed and animated the entire ad in 3D.
