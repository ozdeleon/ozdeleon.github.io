Tommy X Lewis

2019 • Awareness campaign • California

Team

  • Oscar DeLeon, Designer
  • John Mehrkens, Producer

Bringing color and motion to the
Tommy x Lewis lifestyle collection


Tommy x Lewis is a collaboration between Tommy Hilfiger and Lewis Hamilton showcasing Hamilton’s unique personality.

Goal

The goal was to create a mobile ad celebrating both artists in a fun and edgy way.

Challenges

How could I create a dynamic motion design from several static images?

How could I make sure that the design matched the look and feel of the company’s other ads running simultaneously on different platforms?

How do I ensure that Hamilton's PR team approves it on the first go?

Approach

I started by identifying the mood and feel of the campaign, the color elements that made it unique, and graphic assets that set the collection apart from other Tommy Hilfiger collections.

I requested access to all existing ad materials which allowed me to utilize billboards, magazine covers, and video. I isolated the elements and color palettes that were not in the Tommy Hilfiger original brand guidelines to make certain there was a cohesive throughline, as well as harnessing Lewis Hamilton's persona. The last touch was to add syncopated animations to tie in the rest of the campaign's edgy feel.

Plus, Lewis Hamilton's PR team loved it. Win win!
