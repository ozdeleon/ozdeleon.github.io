Indiana University

2019 • Acquisition campaign • California

Team

  • Oscar DeLeon, Designer
  • Melissa Merril, Producer

Helping people move forward


Built motion graphics for IU online's brand new acquisition campaign.

Goals

Create dynamic motion graphics with the intent to drive traffic and interest in becoming a student of Indiana University online.

Challenges

• IU requested motion design, in line with school colors, but provided no assets

• Provided first, middle, and last frames of animation, but IU needed a clearer idea of the motion

Aproach

I pitched ideas and created the storyboards. IU kept rejecting the designs due to basically wanting a completed ad. I then built more in-depth assets with the hope that the client would see the big picture and I could move forward with the campaign. While frustrating at first, it was what eventually led me to my "breakthrough moment". Through creating short animated samples with the purpose of getting the go-ahead from IU, I ended up producing the finished product. They were pretty enthusiastic about the short animations. I was finally able to run with it, delivering a fun and engaging ad.
